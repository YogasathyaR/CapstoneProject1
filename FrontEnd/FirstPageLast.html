How to Recharge Your Plan

Unlock a seamless recharge experience with these detailed steps:

  1. Choose Plans or View Plans After Verified Mobile Number
    Start by entering and verifying your mobile number. Once confirmed, dive into a variety of recharge options—whether it’s data packs, talk time, or special combos—tailored to suit your lifestyle needs.
  2. Select Your Plan
    Browse through the available plans and pick the one that’s just right for you. Each plan comes with clear details—validity, data limits, call benefits—so you can choose what matches your daily rhythm perfectly.
  3. Give Recharge Plan or Buy Plan in Your Wished Plan
    Ready to commit? Hit the “Recharge” or “Buy Now” button on your chosen plan. This step locks in your selection, ensuring you’re one step closer to enjoying uninterrupted services customized to your wishes.
  4. Enter Details in Payment Section
    Head to the payment section and fill in your details—think mobile number, billing info, or any specifics required. It’s quick, secure, and sets the stage for a smooth transaction.
  5. Click Proceed to Payment
    Once your details are in, click “Proceed to Payment.” You’ll be guided to a secure gateway where you can choose your preferred method—credit card, digital wallet, UPI, or more—making it hassle-free and fast.
  6. Confirm Payment
    Double-check your plan and payment details, then hit “Confirm.” Within moments, you’ll receive a success message—your recharge is processed, and you’re all set to enjoy the benefits instantly!
  7. Track Recharge History (Log in as Mobi Comm User)
    Want to keep tabs on your recharges? Log in as a Mobi Comm user to access your recharge history. View past transactions, check plan statuses, and stay in control of your account with ease.
  8. Enjoy Your Plan
    Congratulations! Your plan is active—stream, call, work, or play without a hitch. Revel in the freedom and joy your recharge brings to your everyday life!
